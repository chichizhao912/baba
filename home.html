Dear Baba,

Happy Birthday!!!!

This is the first birthday since I was born that I have no spent by you and mama's side. Although I really wish that I could be with you and mama today, I want you to know that I know that we are still together in our own way. I will try to not feel sad, inside I will feel happy, because every year on the 3rd of September, I feel extra love and warmth from the universe, and I know that we are closer than ever.

Baba, I love you so much. I really love calling you everyday, it makes me feel not lonely, and your opinions are the ones I value the most. I really hope that I can talk to you every day, because ever since I was young, I have always loved listening and talking to you about so many different things.

Thank you and mama for always protecting me and caring for me so much. You are the best mama and baba ever! (Of course).

P.S.

I'm sorry that I can't give you a better birthday gift this year, I will make sure to make up for it in all of our birthdays to come. I hope you and mama really enjoy the cake together.
